1. Introduction

Hammer Girl ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website hammermei.github.io/hammer-girl/ and the associated YouTube channel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services, including our YouTube integration.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize Hammer Girl to access your YouTube account, we collect:

  • YouTube Account Information: Basic channel metadata (name, channel ID) needed to upload content and retrieve analytics.
  • Video Metadata: Titles, descriptions, privacy settings, and thumbnails for videos we upload on your behalf.
  • YouTube Analytics: View counts, watch time, audience retention, and traffic sources for uploaded videos.

We do not collect or store any personal information from visitors to our website. No cookies, no tracking scripts, no analytics — the site is fully static.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we access is used exclusively for:

  • Uploading music videos to the Hammer Girl YouTube channel
  • Updating video metadata (titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
  • Retrieving channel analytics to inform content strategy
  • Managing playlists and video organization

We never share, sell, or rent your information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally in encrypted configuration files and are never transmitted to any server other than Google's official OAuth token endpoints. Access tokens are automatically refreshed and rotated. Revoked tokens are immediately discarded.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties. The YouTube API Services are used solely for the purposes described above. Please see Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us
  • Review what data Google shares with us through your Google Account settings

7. Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google YouTube API Services — video upload, management, and analytics
  • GitHub Pages — static website hosting

Use of YouTube API Services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can review Google's Safety & Security Center at safety.google.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request data deletion, please contact us through the GitHub repository: github.com/HammerMei/hammer-girl.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

This Privacy Policy was created to comply with Google's OAuth API requirements.
